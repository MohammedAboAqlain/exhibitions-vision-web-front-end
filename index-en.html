Our Projects

Some examples of the projects that we have implemented, and always look forward to more projects to make our customers happy from our services.

Colors of Saudi Arabia Forum

Expo Visions is Organized and Managed 31 travelling Exhibition in all part of kingdom of Saudi Arabia in various shopping malls, Exhibition and convention centers for Saudi commissions for Tourism and National Heritage (Colors of Saudi Arabia 2014-2015)

ABSHIR Forum

Expo Visions has been provided Event Registration Service, Self-certificate printing service, Registration Team, Registration Desk Design and production for Saudi Ministry of interior Security Forces Officers Club forum.

Ministry of Water Awareness Campaign

The Saudi Arabia Ministry of Electricity and Water has launched awareness campaign to rationalize water and Electricity consumption in commercial complexes. This campaign was 30 Traveling Exhibition with promoter and documentation (Riyadh, Jeddah, and East Region)

Trina Health Conference

Expo Visions Successfully Organized the Conference for Definition of International Centers for the Care and Treatment of Diabetes (Trina health 2016). Conference for Definition of International Centers for the Care and Treatment of Diabetes (Trina health 2016) organized by Sterile Medical Technology and Expo Visions; collaboration with Saudi charitable Association of diabetes. Conference for Definition of International Centers for the Care and Treatment of Diabetes which was held Riyadh Intercontinental hotel on 19,May,2016 Under the patronage of HRH Prince Faisal Bin Bandar Bin Abdulaziz (Governor Of Riyadh).Achieving all the Excellence and professional atmosphere all our Exhibitors all over the world along with United States, United kingdom, Local Speakers and Great number of visitors.

Riyadh Municipality-Festival

We established Tent Exhibition for Riyadh Municipality-Festival in part of Tent Administration, Main Hall Arrangements, Self Service Devices, Safety System, Gift Items, Printing works. Finally, it was very successful festival.

MOH Tobacco Control

Expo Visions is Manufactured, Installed the Saudi Ministry of Health Tobacco control program booth in Riyadh, Jeddah, Taif and Madina in the commercial complexes.
